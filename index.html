Privacy Policy for Oshud Sheba

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

Welcome to Oshud Sheba, the medicine delivery app operated in Chauddagram Upazila. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, delivery address, prescription details (if uploaded)
  • Device Information: Device ID, OS version
  • Location Information: We collect GPS location (with permission) to identify your delivery location accurately.
  • Call Functionality: When the user taps the “Call Us” button, the app redirects to the phone dialer with our number pre-filled. We do not read call logs or make auto calls.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process medicine orders and deliver them to your address
  • To contact you about your order
  • To verify your delivery location
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Permissions We Request

  • Location Access: To detect your current delivery address
  • Phone Call: To redirect to your phone’s dialer
  • Internet: For online order processing and communication
  • Storage (if applicable): To upload prescription images

4. Data Sharing and Security

Your data is not sold or shared with unauthorized third parties. Data is used strictly for fulfilling orders and improving the service. Authorized delivery and pharmacy partners may access order-specific data only when required.

5. Your Rights

  • Request deletion or correction of your data
  • Withdraw permissions from device settings at any time

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
